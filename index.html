About

The Randle S. Thomas Memorial Scholarship Fund offers Cypress Fairbanks ISD Automotive Technology students who are headed to college or vocational school for auto mechanics related degrees or certification a one time scholarship opportunity. This scholarship fund is for students with financial need who display academic achievement, a commitment to serving others (in school, their community, or at home), a strong work ethic, and leadership qualities.

Randle Scott Thomas, of Houston, Texas, was born on December 19, 1962, and passed away on January 30, 2022.

Randle just recently retired from Cypress-Fairbanks Independent School District. He was an Auto-Tech Instructor at Cy-Falls High School for 18 years, he started the program at Cy-Falls. He was a sponsor of the Cy-Falls Men of Honor Club. Prior to that, he was an Auto-Tech Instructor for San Jacinto College for 17 years.

Randle was also involved in the Boy Scouts of America, Sam Houston Area Council. He was Cub Master of Pack 771, Committee Chairman for Troop 277 and was involved with the Colonneh Lodge Order of the Arrow and Soaring Eagle District. In leiu of flowers, Randle requested a Scholarship Fund be set up for Cypress-Fairbanks I.S.D. Automotive Students to further their education in automotive technology.
